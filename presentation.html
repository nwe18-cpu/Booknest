Background & Goals

Concept
  • Modern Retail Challenge: Legacy online bookstores suffer from generic, unpolished layouts, confusing navigation, and cluttered interfaces.
  • The Booknest Vision: Establish a premium bookstore experience online using a customized, unified HSL color design layout (Cozy Green & Gold).
  • Administrative Efficiency: Give operators a dedicated control panel to track inventory, fulfill customer orders, and enforce security policies.

Design Focus

"To capture the relaxing scent of real paper bookstands, utilizing rich forest green gradients, warm gold highlights, and clean typography."

Technical Stack

Architecture
  • Backend Powerhouse: Powered by Laravel 10 using structural MVC architecture pattern.
  • Data Integrity: Structured Relational DB schema storing transactions, accounts, catalogs, and logs.
  • Clean Frontend Philosophy: Created with pure HTML5, Vanilla JS, and normalized CSS. Zero reliance on bloated frontend packages (Bootstrap/Tailwind).
  • Security Foundations: Implements secure staff session guards, password hashing, validation rules, and CSRF token protections.

Staff Management & Security

Role Guards
  • Role Authorization: Safeguards system by verifying roles. Only Admin role can edit business operations; Staff role is restricted.
  • Staff Directory CRUD: Admin dashboard allows full management of staff access logs, creation, and profile edits.
  • Self-Deletion Safeguard: Validation rules prevent active administrators from deleting their own login accounts.
Admin Holds Full CRUD & Business Actions
Staff Restricted to Catalog & Author Management
